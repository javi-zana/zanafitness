## Core mental models worth memorizing

1. **Trust = bank account.** Deposits (value, stories) before withdrawals (CTAs, sales). Don't ask before you've given. (full-summary.md:805)
2. **Lead temperature = response time + length.** Fast+long=hot, slow+short=cold. Mirror their energy. (full-summary.md:2110)
3. **You are the niche.** People follow people, not topics. Personality + story is the moat. (full-summary.md:1245, 1736)
4. **4 sale conditions:** Has problem → aware of it → knows/likes/trusts you → urgency. Launch must hit all four. (full-summary.md:4205)
5. **Selling = serving.** If your program helps, it's your responsibility to offer it. Never apologize. (full-summary.md:280, 2547)
6. **Closing 40% vs 20% doubles revenue.** Sales skill is the highest-leverage skill. (full-summary.md:626)
7. **Treat soft-launch clients as VIPs, not betas.** Overdelivery in this phase = testimonials = next launch fuel. (full-summary.md:4100)
