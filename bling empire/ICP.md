# Ideal Client Profile (ICP)

**Final Statement:** I help Asian male professionals, 25-34 build a lean, aesthetic physique that matches their lifestyle — through a habits-based lifestyle system.

**Clean one-liner:** "Confident, lean, aesthetic, Asian build — through lifestyle habits."

## Demographics
- **Age:** 25–34
- **Gender:** Male
- **Ethnicity:** Asian (Singapore, Indonesia, Philippines, Indian, Malaysian)
- **Occupation:** White-collar / entrepreneurs — finance, tech, consulting, law, creative/agency, early-stage entrepreneurs, family-business next-gen
- **Income:** $3.5K–5K USD/mo. Coaching at $500–$1,200 is casual-to-meaningful, not a stretch
- **Relationship:** Mostly single or serious relationships (dating-to-early-marriage). Few kids. Still has time for themselves.

## Fitness Profile
- **Experience:** Beginner to Intermediate (lift inconsistently or tried programs and fallen off)
- **Primary goal:** Body recomposition — lean, defined, aesthetic build
- **Targets:** Drop to ~12–15% body fat, build visible shoulders/chest/arms ("looks good in a fitted shirt / polo / suit"), maintain year-round without extreme dieting or 2-hour gym sessions

## Pain Points
1. No idea what to eat — don't understand how to approach diet
2. Can't stick to the diet plan
3. Don't know which gym split to run, pre/post workout habits
4. Supplement confusion
5. It never fits their life — 2-3 free hours a day, flexible schedules
6. Look "skinny-fat" or "soft" in clothes despite earning well and dressing well — physique doesn't match lifestyle

## Motivations & Desires

**Surface:** Lose belly fat, build shoulders/chest/arms, look good shirtless on vacation, fit into tailored clothing, look good in photos

**Deeper drivers:**
- **Status and respect** — walk into a room and have people see them as someone who has their life together. Body is the last missing piece.
- **Control** — built a career, income, life. Body is the one domain that still feels chaotic.
- **Quiet confidence** — not gym-bro vanity. The guy who clearly takes care of himself without making a thing of it.
- **Attraction** — being desired by their partner or the kind of partner they want.
- **Longevity** — awareness that 30s/40s are coming, don't want to be the soft uncle.

**Future vision:** A version of themselves where the aesthetic is effortless, habitual, and where the body is a quiet asset that compounds with everything else — career, style, relationships, network.

## Differentiator
Getting ripped was one of the highest ROI things Javi ever did: it let him be an influencer, amplified him in business, gave him confidence that manifests in "attract" type content. The program focuses on habits and principles ingrained in everyday meals, workouts, and recovery — not focused on the gym itself, but the lifestyle that surrounds it.
